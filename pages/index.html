Nicolas Filliol

Electrical Engineering Student at ETH Zurich

I am a 21 years old student from Switzerland, currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology at the ETH Zurich. This website serves as an overview to my most recent projects.

I have always been obsessed with the idea to tackle real world problems and solve them with the help of computers and technologies wherefore I decided to pursue a degree in electrical engineering at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Zurich. Yet, my interests have always gone well beyond just the electrical aspects of engineering. Specifically, computer science and programming, with its cutting-edge technologies such as Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, have grabbed my attention.

Nevertheless, the hardware aspects of engineering stay highly relevant to me. The ability to understand how electrical devices work, how to use electricity to power machines and build computers, and how to process signals efficiently have convinced me to study electrical engineering and to head towards robotics afterwards. I am constantly attempting to implement in practice the theory conveyed in my studies by carrying out my own projects with tools such as Arduino and Raspberry Pi.
